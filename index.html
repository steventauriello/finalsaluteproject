How It Works

1

Nominate a Veteran

Family, friends, or the veteran themselves can submit a nomination with basic service details and a short story about their career.

2

We Select Recipients

Each month, we select veterans based on available funds, materials, and current workload, focusing on those recently retired or nearing retirement.

3

We Build the Crate

A handcrafted wooden keepsake crate is built and customized with their name, rank, branch, and other details that matter to them.

4

We Ship It Free

The finished crate is carefully packaged and shipped to the veteran or their family at no cost as a final salute to their service.
